# Scientific Agent Research 2025-2026

Scientific-agent research is transitioning from prototypes to benchmarked systems with reproducibility and governance constraints.

## Focus Areas

1. End-to-end research pipelines: hypothesis, experiment design, analysis, report drafting.
2. Domain-specific tooling and environment interfaces.
3. Evaluation of methodological validity, not only textual quality.
4. Oversight and governance for high-impact scientific workflows.

## Implementation Implications

1. Prompt contracts must encode evidentiary standards and uncertainty handling.
2. Agent traces should preserve methodological decisions for review.
3. Evaluation requires domain-expert rubrics and reproducibility checks.

## Practical Mapping in This Wiki

Use these works to inform high-rigor agent deployments in coding, analytics, and research-heavy domains.
